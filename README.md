# CoreQuest

**Same model. Wildly different results. The difference is the partnership.**

Most people know how to use a pencil. Da Vinci knew how to use a pencil. The difference was never the pencil.

CoreQuest is a free, structured workshop method for supercharging your Human/AI partnership. It captures what your team knows but no document ever records — the trust gaps, career stakes, and product truth your AI can't see — and turns it into the context that makes every AI output better.

**Live site:** [www.corequest.net](https://www.corequest.net)

## Why this exists

Your company's AI tools write fluently from whatever context they're given. But corporate data has a survivorship bias: it only contains traces of people who engaged. There is no transcript of a conversation that didn't happen — the prospect who never clicked because a trust-building step was missed, the career stakes that actually drive buying decisions, the difference between what ships today and what's on the roadmap. That insight lives in your people's heads, and it is now the scarce input to everything your AI produces.

CoreQuest is the disciplined process for getting it out — with credit for the people who contribute it built in from the start.

## Three ways to run it

1. **Solo or small team, right now:** copy the [AI Moderator Prompt](downloads/corequest-ai-moderator-prompt.md) into Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini and run the full eleven-worksheet sequence with an AI moderator.
2. **As a team, asynchronously:** use [CoreQuest Session](https://www.corequest.net/session.html) — every answer is recorded with its author's name automatically, and the AI Context Pack comes out with provenance assembled. No accounts, no server; your session lives in a file your team owns.
3. **Cross-functional, live:** download the kit below and run the classic moderated workshop. The [Moderator's Guide](downloads/corequest-moderators-guide.md) covers participant selection, engagement, room politics, and credit.
