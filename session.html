The Async Team Workshop · No accounts · No server · Nothing uploaded

Every contribution, credited automatically.

Run the CoreQuest sequence with your team, asynchronously. Each answer is recorded with its author's name and time the moment it's written — credit enforced by format, not by policy. Your session lives in a file your team owns; nothing here is sent anywhere.

How the async flow works: ① Enter your name and add your answers. ② Export the session file and put it in your team's shared drive (or send it on). ③ The next person imports it, reads everyone's attributed answers — often the best prompt for their own — and adds theirs, building on others where it clicks. ④ When the round feels done, generate the outputs below. Importing several files at once merges parallel rounds automatically.

Running this with a team? The Session Guide covers both team workflows — the relay and the parallel fan-out — plus an AI merge prompt that consolidates duplicate answers from parallel sessions and combines the credit.
